About NKU WiCys Chapter:

WiCys is a group that supports women in advancing and retaining positions in Cybersecurity. WiCys offers unique training, networking, and scholarships to its members. It is open to all students. WiCyS has a dedicated Job Board, a bi-annual virtual career, and a physical career fair at WiCyS annual conference where numerous recruiters post their open CSEC roles all around the year.At least one Chapter representative will be awarded a student scholarship to attend the WiCyS Annual Conference.

Events

Collaboration with iSPACE: The STEM Learning Place

We teamed up with iSPACE to host Cyber Spy Night, diving into cybersecurity with activities on cryptography, digital forensics, and coding. The girls started by creating their own Caesar Cipher Wheel, then took part in a forensics-themed scavenger hunt, and finished by coding a Caesar Cipher program in Python at the Cyber Threat Lab to learn how technology defends against cyber threats.





WiCys National Conference 2024

We sent three members of our chapter to the WiCyS National Conference, the premier event dedicated to recruiting, retaining, and advancing women in cybersecurity. This conference fosters a supportive community, providing engagement, encouragement, and networking opportunities for women and allies in the field.
